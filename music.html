MUSIC

'Friends' is the debut single from Reasons to live, released on July 4th 2025 on Spotify and most other streaming services. This track is a heartfelt tribute and honour to friendship, blending infectious house beats with carefully crafted samples from Broder Daniel's iconic song Luke Skywalker - with a blessing from Henrik Bergren himself.

With its joyous energy and gritty, raw edge, 'Friends' captures the spirit of connection and celebration. If you love house music that's both dirty and uplifting, this is a tune you don't want to miss.

Turn it up, feel the warmth, and let 'Friends' remind you why friendship is one of life's greatest reasons to dance. Enjoy it with your friends, partners, or simply on your own. Have a good summer - see you soon!
